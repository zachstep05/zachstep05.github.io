Whiskey and Coke cake

Bolognese sauce

20/02/19

Perfect for spaghetti! Great to freeze.

Difficulty: hard
Makes 8-10 servings



INGREDIENTS
12 tablespoons butter
2 Cups onion (coarsely chopped
1 Cup celery (coarsely chopped)
1/2 Cup carrots (coarsely chopped
2 pounds meat (mix chuck + lean pork 2:3)
1 Cup white wine
2 Cups milk
2 pounds tomatoes (finely chopped, may substitute with finely chopped canned plum)
2 tablespoons parsley, finely chopped
Salt, pepper, nutmeg to taste


INSTRUCTIONS

Brown all the meat, remove the fat, set aside
Melt butter in large stainless pan
Add onion, celery and carrots
Cook 10 mins, stirring frequently
Add meat, mash into vegetables until all clumps gone
Raise heat, cook 3-5 minutes
Add wine, boil and stir constantly until wine is dissolved
Stir in milk, keep stirring until dissolved
Add tomatoes, bring sauce to boil, reduce heat to lowest
half cover pan, simmer for 3 hours stirring now and then
Add parsley, salt, pepper and nutmeg

serve warm or freeze for later.
