Stephens almond cookie recipe

29/12/18

This recipe was created by my great grandma. when I tried them for the first time they were so delicious that I had to make my own batch the next day! I wanted to share this recipe, so I decided to post her secret formula.

Makes 2-3 dozen

INGREDIENTS
2 and 1/4 cup flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/8 teaspoon salt
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup granulated sugar
1 and 1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
2 teaspoons almond extract
1 cup almonds, slivered.


INSTRUCTIONS
PREHEAT oven to 180°C/350°F
BEAT butter and sugars until creamy.
ADD eggs and almond extract; beat well.
ADD combined flour, baking powder and salt, mix well.
STIR IN almonds, mix well.
DROP by rounded spoons (2-5 inch diameter) onto ungreased baking sheets.


BAKE for 9 to 10 minutes.
COOL on baking sheets for 1 minute; remove to wire rack.
